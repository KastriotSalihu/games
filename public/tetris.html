How to play

    Use arrows to move tetromino around
    Press 'R' to rotate tetromino
    Hold Shift when pressing 'R' to rotate counterclockwise
    Press 'P' to pause the game
    Press 'T' to fix skyline
    Press the space bar to place current tetromino where it is, and add new one
