Welcome to Simon Says! In this game, you will see four colored buttons: red, green, blue, and yellow. To play, just watch the game as it plays a sequence of colors for you. Then, repeat that sequence by clicking the buttons in the same order.

If you correctly repeat the sequence, the game will add another color and play the longer sequence again. As you play, the game will get more difficult by adding more colors to the sequence. But be careful! If you make a mistake and click the wrong button, the game will end and you'll have to start over.

To start playing, just click the 'Start' button. Then, pay attention to the sequence of colors and repeat them by clicking the buttons in the same order. If you get stuck or need help, don't worry - the game will give you feedback if you make a mistake, such as by flashing the correct sequence or displaying a message.

The goal of the game is to repeat the longest possible sequence of colors. Can you beat your high score and become a Simon Says champion? Give it a try and find out!
